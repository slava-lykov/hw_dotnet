Preconditions:
1. User authorized on booking.com
2. User placed on the main booking.com page

Case 1
- Type name of the place to the 'Where are you going?' field
- Select 'Check-in' and 'Check-out' dates for future in the 'Check-in' and 'Check-out' field
- Select the count of Adults/Children/Rooms in the 'Adults/Children/Rooms' field
- Select one of the provided item
- Select room
- Click to the Reserve button
- Type all of needed information
- Confirme booking of the room

Results:
1. Room is booked 
2. There is email notification
